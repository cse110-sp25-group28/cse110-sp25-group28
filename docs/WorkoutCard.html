Class: WorkoutCard

WorkoutCard()

Custom element representing a workout card. Displays workout details on the front and the muscle group on the back. Can be flipped unless flipping is disabled (e.g., in selection mode).

disableFlip

Enables or disables flipping for the card. When set to true, the card cannot be flipped and always shows the workout (face up). When set to false, the card can be flipped.
